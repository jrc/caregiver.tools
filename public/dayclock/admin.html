Customize Your Clock

You can set a custom message to be displayed at the bottom of the Day Clock.

You can write [YYYY-MM-DD] with a future date and it will be shown in natural language, such as "tomorrow" or "in 5 days". You can also write [MON], [TUE], etc. to show the next occurrence of that weekday.

The Day Clock updates hourly. If you want to load the new message immediately, you can double-tap on the Day Clock.
