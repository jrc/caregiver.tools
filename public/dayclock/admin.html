Customize Your Clock

Set a custom message to be displayed at the bottom of the Day Clock. Leave blank for no message.

  • The Day Clock looks for new messages hourly. To make it update immediately, double-tap on the Day Clock device's screen.
  • You can write dates in square brackets and they will be displayed in natural language, like "tomorrow" or "in 3 days". For example: [2025-07-01]
